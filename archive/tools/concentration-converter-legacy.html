This page helps you understand how molecular weight connects mass concentration and molar concentration.

Scientific question

When a molecule changes from ng/mL or mg/mL to nM or μM, what relationship is doing the conversion?

Scientific idea

Mass concentration describes how much material is present. Molar concentration describes how many molecules or moles are present. Molecular weight is the bridge between them.

Mentor note

The same mass concentration can mean very different molar exposure for molecules with different molecular weights. Check the conversion before comparing assays or dose-response ranges.
