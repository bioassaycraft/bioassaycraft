# 为什么会有 BioassayCraft？

BioassayCraft 的诞生，源于一个很简单的观察。

药典、法规和指南告诉我们应该做什么。

但真正的实践，往往需要回答另一个问题：

**为什么这样做？**

在 Bioassay 工作中，真正困难的往往不是找到答案，而是理解答案背后的科学依据。

为什么选择这个模型？

为什么需要这个统计检验？

为什么实验要这样设计？

为什么同样符合药典要求的方法，在实践中的表现却可能完全不同？

这些问题，很少能够从法规本身获得完整答案。

而我们长期从事 Bioassay 方法开发与验证工作，也深知理论与实践之间始终存在着一段距离。

BioassayCraft 希望弥补这段距离。

它不是药典的替代品，也不是法规指南的翻译。

它希望成为**理论、法规、统计与实验实践之间的一座桥梁**。

帮助使用者不仅知道应该怎么做，更理解为什么这样做。

---

# 我们相信

Bioassay 不是一套固定流程。

它是一段不断思考、不断验证、不断做出科学判断的旅程。

真正重要的，不只是得到正确答案。

更重要的是理解答案为什么正确。

因为每一次模型选择、每一次统计分析、每一次实验设计，本质上都是一次科学判断。

BioassayCraft 希望让这些判断变得**可见、可探索、可理解**。

我们希望帮助使用者建立科学判断，而不是依赖工具本身。

希望大家离开 BioassayCraft 之后，仍然能够独立思考、独立分析、独立解决问题。

因为真正值得被带走的，从来不是某一个结果。

而是理解结果的能力。

BioassayCraft 长期坚持一些简单而明确的原则。

- 理论应该连接实践，而不是停留在理论。
- 交互应该揭示推理，而不是隐藏复杂性。
- 可视化应该帮助理解，而不是追求装饰。

- 真正应该培养的，是科学判断，而不是对工具的依赖。

这些原则不仅指导网站设计。

也指导每一个页面、每一个交互、每一个 Journey，以及每一个 Tool 的诞生。

# Why does BioassayCraft exist?

BioassayCraft began with a simple observation.

Pharmacopoeias, guidelines, and regulations explain what should be done.

Real scientific work asks a different question:

**Why?**

In bioassay, the hardest part is rarely finding an answer.

The real challenge is understanding the reasoning behind it.

Why is this model chosen?

Why is this statistical test necessary?

Why should an experiment be designed this way?

Why can two methods that both satisfy regulatory expectations behave so differently in practice?

These questions are rarely answered by the guidelines themselves.

Having worked in bioassay development and validation for years, we have experienced this gap firsthand.

BioassayCraft was created to bridge that gap.

Not by replacing pharmacopoeias.

Not by rewriting guidelines.

But by becoming a bridge between scientific theory, regulatory guidance, statistics, and laboratory practice.

Helping scientists understand not only **what** to do, but **why**.

---

# What we believe

Bioassay is not a fixed procedure.

It is a journey of questioning, testing, and scientific judgement.

Getting the correct answer is important.

Understanding why it is correct is even more important.

Every model selection, statistical analysis, and experimental design is ultimately a scientific decision.

BioassayCraft aims to make those decisions **visible, explorable, and understandable**.

Rather than encouraging dependence on the platform, we hope to cultivate independent scientific judgement.

Our goal is that users leave with stronger reasoning, greater confidence, and the ability to solve new problems on their own.

Because what truly matters is never the answer itself.

It is the ability to understand it.

---

# Learn

Learning comes first.

Learn is not a digital textbook, nor a collection of regulatory summaries.

It explains the ideas behind practice.

It connects pharmacopoeias, statistics, modelling, experimental design, and scientific reasoning into one coherent learning experience.

Every topic exists to help users understand real scientific work.

Not only the theory itself.

But how theory becomes practice.

---

# Journey

Understanding naturally leads to practice.

Journey is not a checklist.

It represents the way real bioassay projects evolve.

From method development to validation, data interpretation, and lifecycle management.

Users are not simply guided through pages.

They are guided through a sequence of real scientific decisions.

Journey is not intended to make decisions for people.

It is designed to help them understand why each decision matters—and what alternatives may exist.

---

# Tools

Tools are the final part of BioassayCraft.

But they are never the final goal.

Every calculator, simulator, and interactive visualization should do more than produce numbers.

They should help users understand:

What does this result mean?

Where does it come from?

What assumptions support it?

When can it be trusted?

When should it be questioned?

**Every calculation should teach.**

Because the value is never the number itself.

It is the scientific meaning behind the number.

---

# Design Principles

BioassayCraft is guided by a few simple principles.

- Connect theory with practice.
- Reveal reasoning through interaction.
- Use visualization to improve understanding, never as decoration.
- Build tools that teach instead of merely calculating.
- Respect pharmacopoeias without treating them as the end of scientific thinking.
- Cultivate scientific judgement rather than dependence on software.

These principles guide every page, every interaction, every Journey, and every Tool within BioassayCraft.

BioassayCraft aims to become a thoughtful, practical, and scientifically grounded learning platform.

We hope people enjoy using BioassayCraft not because it offers many tools, but because it makes them feel that their questions are understood, that practical challenges are respected, and that complex ideas are explained with care.


Better science begins with deeper understanding.

What is truly worth passing on is never just the answer.

It is the ability to understand.

BioassayCraft is not built simply to provide answers.
